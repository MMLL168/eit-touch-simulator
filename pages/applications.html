Overview

Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT) touch sensing represents a breakthrough in transparent, flexible, and cost-effective touch technology. This page explores six major application domains, compares EIT with alternative technologies, provides detailed cost-benefit analysis, and presents comprehensive performance benchmarks.
